# Staff Rostering System - Management Overview

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** November 20, 2025  
**Audience:** Apex Management  
**Purpose:** What We Are Building

---

## Executive Summary

We are building an AI-driven workforce scheduling platform that automates roster creation, enforces labor compliance, optimizes labor costs, and empowers employees through mobile-first self-service. The system will support 10-50 retail stores across multiple formats (grocery/supermarket, boutique/fashion) with a mixed workforce of 200-1,000 employees (FTE + contractors).

**Key Outcomes:**
- **70% reduction** in manager scheduling time (from 7 hours to <2 hours per week)
- **Zero labor law violations** through automated compliance enforcement
- **±3% labor cost variance** from budget through real-time tracking and alerts
- **98%+ operational task completion** for grocery stores through task-based scheduling
- **Rapid response to disruptions** through real-time gap detection and actionable recommendations for absences and demand surges

---

## System Capabilities

### 1. Automated Schedule Generation
**What it does:** AI engine creates optimal, conflict-free rosters in under 30 seconds

**Key Features:**
- Matches employees to shifts based on skills, qualifications, and availability
- Enforces employee contract hours (FTE vs. part-time limits)
- Prevents double-booking and scheduling conflicts
- Applies templates for recurring schedule patterns
- Integrates demand forecasting with operational task requirements

**Business Value:** Eliminates 80% of manual planning time while ensuring optimal staffing

---

### 2. Task & Activity-Based Scheduling
**What it does:** Calculates staffing needs from operational tasks (deliveries, stocking, cleaning) plus customer service demand

**Key Features:**
- Defines recurring operational tasks with required manpower and timing
- Prioritizes tasks (P0/P1/P2) with guaranteed staffing for critical activities
- Matches staff to tasks based on specific skill requirements
- Configures per store format (enabled for grocery/supermarket; optional for boutique)
- Tracks task completion in real-time with compliance monitoring

**Business Value:** Prevents operational failures while optimizing labor allocation across tasks and customer service

---

### 3. Demand-Based Forecasting
**What it does:** Predicts staffing needs based on historical sales and foot traffic patterns

**Key Features:**
- Analyzes historical sales data to identify patterns
- Adjusts for seasonal events and holidays
- Identifies peak hours requiring additional staff
- Provides recommended staffing levels by role and store
- Integrates with task-based scheduling for unified manpower planning

**Business Value:** Prevents understaffing (lost sales) and overstaffing (wasted labor cost)

---

### 4. Labor Cost Management & Budgeting
**What it does:** Real-time tracking of labor spend against budgets with proactive alerts

**Key Features:**
- Sets weekly/monthly labor budgets per store
- Projects costs in real-time while building schedules
- Alerts managers to overtime before shift approval
- Supports different pay rates (FTE vs. contractors, regular vs. overtime)
- Tracks labor cost as percentage of revenue

**Business Value:** Direct P&L impact by preventing budget overruns before they occur

---

### 5. Compliance Engine
**What it does:** Automated monitoring and enforcement of labor laws, awards, and company policies

**Key Features:**
- Enforces maximum work hours (daily/weekly limits)
- Validates minimum rest periods between shifts
- Ensures mandatory break requirements
- Protects minor labor restrictions (child labor laws)
- Respects contract term limits
- Immutable audit trail for compliance reporting

**Business Value:** Legal risk mitigation; eliminates violation risk and associated fines

---

### 6. Real-Time Roster Intelligence & Recommendations
**What it does:** Proactive gap detection, alerts, and actionable recommendations for roster disruptions

**Key Features:**
- **Absence Detection:** Detects when employees don't clock in at scheduled time; alerts manager within minutes
- **Gap Analysis:** Identifies coverage gaps in real-time (no-show, late arrival, early departure)
- **Task Redistribution:** Suggests how to redistribute absent employee's tasks among available staff
- **Replacement Recommendations:** Recommends eligible employees to call in (available, qualified, within hours)
- **Demand Surge Detection:** Monitors real-time foot traffic/sales data for sudden increases
- **Surge Response:** Suggests immediate actions (call backup, extend shifts, redistribute tasks)
- **Forecasted Gaps:** Predicts potential coverage gaps and recommends preventive actions
- **One-Click Actions:** Enables managers to approve recommendations with single click

**Business Value:** Prevents operational disruptions while enabling rapid response and reducing crisis decision-making time

---

## User Roles & Capabilities

### **Store Manager**
**What they can do:**
- **Schedule Creation:** Auto-generate optimal rosters in <30 seconds; review and adjust; publish schedules
- **Task Management:** Define operational tasks; monitor task completion in real-time; receive alerts for at-risk tasks
- **Labor Cost Control:** View real-time cost projections; receive overtime alerts; track budget variance
- **Compliance Oversight:** System blocks violations automatically; review compliance reports
- **Employee Management:** Approve shift swaps; approve leave requests; manage availability blackouts
- **Real-Time Roster Intelligence:** Receive instant alerts for absences, no-shows, and demand surges; get actionable recommendations (task redistribution, replacement suggestions); approve recommendations with one click
- **Reporting:** Access store-level labor cost, attendance, and task completion reports
- **Mobile Access:** Full manager capabilities via mobile app with real-time alerts

**Key Workflows:**
- **Schedule Creation:** Log in → View demand forecast + tasks → Click "Auto-generate" → Review (2-3 min) → Publish → Employees notified automatically
- **Real-Time Response:** Receive alert (employee no-show or demand surge) → View recommendations (redistribute tasks OR call replacement) → Approve with one click → System updates roster automatically

---

### **Assistant Manager**
**What they can do:**
- **Schedule Review:** View and suggest edits to schedules (requires manager approval)
- **Task Monitoring:** Track operational task completion; flag issues
- **Time & Attendance:** Approve timesheet edits; flag exceptions
- **Shift Coverage:** Approve shift swaps (if delegated)
- **Real-Time Alerts:** Receive alerts for roster gaps; view recommendations; escalate to manager if needed
- **Employee Support:** Assist employees with app usage; handle basic requests

---

### **Employee**
**What they can do:**
- **Schedule Access:** View personal schedule (web/mobile); receive push notifications for changes
- **Clock In/Out:** GPS-verified time tracking via mobile app
- **Shift Management:** Request shift swaps; bid on open shifts; set availability preferences
- **Leave Requests:** Submit time off requests; view leave balances
- **Task Tracking:** Mark operational tasks as "In Progress" → "Completed" (grocery stores)
- **Self-Service:** Update personal details; view historical timesheets

**Key Workflow:** Open mobile app → View schedule → Receive shift reminder → Clock in (GPS verified) → Complete tasks → Clock out → Timesheet auto-generated

---

### **Regional Manager**
**What they can do:**
- **Multi-Store Visibility:** View aggregate reports across all stores in region
- **Budget Oversight:** Monitor labor cost variance across stores
- **Compliance Monitoring:** Review compliance reports for all stores
- **Performance Analysis:** Compare store-level metrics (attendance, task completion, cost efficiency)
- **Policy Configuration:** Set region-specific policies and budgets

---

### **HR/Admin**
**What they can do:**
- **Employee Master Data:** Manage employee profiles, skills, certifications
- **Compliance Configuration:** Set labor law rules per location
- **System Configuration:** Configure store types, task templates, pay rates
- **Reporting:** Access organization-wide reports; export data for payroll
- **User Management:** Manage user access and roles

---

### **Finance/Accounting**
**What they can do:**
- **Budget Management:** Set and adjust labor budgets per store/period
- **Cost Analysis:** View labor cost reports; track % of revenue
- **Integration Management:** Monitor payroll system integration; verify timesheet exports
- **Financial Reporting:** Export labor cost data to ERP/accounting systems
